Overview

KnowledgeWright Sales is a tool for capturing the expertise of your top sales, pre-sales technical, and marketing people and deploying it on your Web site.

Problem

The problem with technical sales on the Internet is that a prospect must interpret the product presentations and suitability of the product for his/her particular situation. The valuable ability of a skilled sales person to map their needs and situation to the benefits and features of the product is missing.

Solution

Technical benefits and feature relationships are maintained in a knowledgebase that is used to dynamically generate a custom dialog with the user, probing for needs and pattern-matching them against the benefits and features of the system.

The prospect gets the sort of personal attention, and custom recommendations that would normally only be achieved through direct human contact.

In addition to dynamically generating a dialog, and explaining benefits, the system generates a proposal, clearly laying out what the prospect should do next based on those needs and situation.

Benefits

  • Reduces frustration of Web-site visitors.
  • Allows precision presentation of sales benefits to Web-visitors.
  • Provides links to the key parts of a Web-site that should be visited.
  • Generates proposals dynamically targeted for each visitor.
  • Saves log of interactive sessions, providing valuable marketing information.
